The csv files are the examples of reference and synthetic well data used as a input file of the PINN program. 
X_Easting: Coordinates in X direction
Y_Northing: Coordindates in Y direction
Elevation: Corrdinates in Z direction
T_degC: Temperature in degree celsius 
P_Pa: Pore pressure in Pa
log10PER: Logarithm of permeability
